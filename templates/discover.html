Weather prediction

Natural Disasters

Global Warming

Glossary

Here is a glossary of technical terms you may find while using this website

Pipeline A pipeline is a term used across many industries to describe an organised plan, with a start and end date, involving a series of stages.
In this context, we are looking for satellite images between two dates, hopefully delivering results within specified intervals. AOI The AOI, or Area of Interest, is the determined location on the surface of the Earth, of which you hope to receive satellite images. AOI coverage The AOI coverage refers to the percentage of the AOI which was selected which could be captured by satellite imaging technology.
All pipelines created on our platform gurantee that all images will have captured at least 50% of the AOI selected. Archived imagery Imagery which has already been captured and downloaded by satellites. If your pipeline involves any intervals which will start and finish before today will deliver archived images.
